Digital Privacy Guide for Teens

Learn how to build a safe, smart, and positive digital identity online.

Positive Digital Identity

  • Think before you post – words and pictures last forever
  • Use real names only when necessary
  • Support others online with positive comments
  • Share accomplishments and hobbies to build a good image

Source: Common Sense Media – commonsense.org

Protecting Your Privacy

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Think twice before sharing your location
  • Review app permissions and privacy settings

Source: StaySafeOnline.org

Be a Smart Digital Citizen

  • Don't click suspicious links or download unknown files
  • Understand what data websites collect
  • Use privacy-focused browsers like Brave or Firefox
  • Think critically about what you read online

Source: Digital Citizenship Institute – digitalcitizenship.net
